Ancient Culinary History

The History of Persian Food

Spanning over 2,500 years, Persian cuisine is a tapestry of the Silk Road. It balanced the "Garm" (hot) and "Sard" (cold) nature of foods to ensure the body remained in harmony—a precursor to modern nutritional balance.

Greek Culinary Heritage

In Ancient Greece, food was a philosophy. They believed in "Let food be thy medicine." Their focus on olive oil, legumes, and honey created the blueprint for the healthiest diet known to man: The Mediterranean Diet.

The Evolution of Restaurants

From Roman "Thermopolia" serving warm wine and lentils to travelers, to the 18th-century Parisian "Bouillons" that served restorative broths, the restaurant has always been a place of healing and restoration.
